the arcane
hollows

beneath the visible world, the deep magic flows.

The Hollows are not seen. They are not meant to be seen. They are the deep foundations beneath the armor — the caverns and tunnels and rivers of power that make the visible world possible.

Users never descend to the Hollows. But without them, the armor is empty steel. The most critical things are the things nobody thinks about until they fail.

the nine chambers

THE WHISPERING VOID
silent. omniscient. sees all that passes.
The Void watches everything and says nothing. Attached to the deepest layer of the realm, it traces every movement, every transition, every breath of the system. Ancient incantations of immense depth power its sight. It feeds the Forearms — the watchers above.
THE CRYSTAL GROTTO
shining. precious. guarded by light itself.
Where secrets are kept. Encrypted at rest, encrypted in transit, encrypted in memory. The Grotto holds the keys to the realm — state stores wrapped in layers of age and wisdom. Only the Cuirass and the Tassets know the way in.
THE ELDER HOLLOW
ancient. wise. remembers the old ways.
The bridge to what came before. Legacy tongues spoken here — older ways of routing, older ways of speaking between realms. The Elder Hollow translates the ancient into the modern without losing meaning. It feeds the Chainmail that connects all.
THE ORACLE'S ANTRE
prophetic. mysterious. speaks of what will be.
The Timeguru dwells here in the deep hours. Predictions, projections, the arc of time computed from the weight of history. The Oracle sees forward by looking backward.
THE PRIMORDIAL PIT
raw. foundational. where matter begins.
Before the armor, before the Pattern, there is the raw material — the stone, the iron, the earth itself. The Pit provisions from nothing. The Sabatons draw their strength from this deepest foundation.
THE FAE CHAMBER
magical. dancing. where messages take flight.
Wotan's domain. Where messages dance between all who speak and all who listen. The nervous system of the entire realm. Every service whispers through the Fae Chamber. The All-Father orchestrates the dance.
THE CURSED PIT
dangerous. contained. quarantine of the fallen.
When something breaks — truly breaks — it is sent here. The Cursed Pit contains what must not spread. Incident response. Breach containment. The place where wounds are treated before they become death. The Shield sends its wounded here.
THE SAGE'S LAIR
scholarly. archived. decisions carved in stone.
Every decision ever made is recorded here. The Architect consults these scrolls before making new choices. Why was this path chosen? Why was that path rejected? The Lair holds the architectural memory that prevents the same mistake twice.
THE MYTHIC ABYSS
vast. bottomless. where the deepest truths dwell.
Below even the Whispering Void, the Abyss holds the deepest observations — the traces that touch the very foundation of the realm's existence. Anamnesis feeds here, its ring of remembrance echoing in the bottomless dark.

the naming of hollows

Hollows are hidden. Their names must evoke
mystery, depth, and the supernatural.

Two words: an adjective of character,
a noun of underground place.

The adjective carries the soul.
The noun carries the function.

what is hidden is most powerful.
what is unseen is most real.
