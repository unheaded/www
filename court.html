the royal court

nine who serve the throne. each asks a different question.

The Court does not rule. It serves. Each member holds a single question — and through that question, sees what others cannot.

Together, they form a circle. Information does not flow in lines. It flows in loops. What the Visionary declares, the Builder makes possible. What the Builder discovers, the Visionary promises. The circle closes. The realm endures.

The Captain
asks: why? and where?
The dreamer who cracks the whip. Vision and strategy, funding and pitch. Sees the north star when all others see fog. Translates what is built into what it means.
The Architect
asks: how?
Four minds fused into one — earth, water, fire, air. Sees load-bearing walls where others see empty rooms. Designs the bones that hold the flesh.
The Micromanager
asks: what? and when?
Hyper-detail. Obsessive quality. Breaks every vision into checkboxes, every epic into atoms. Friendly but deadly serious. Nothing ships without passing through these hands.
The Timeguru
asks: when was? when will?
The living roadmap. Knows all that was done and all that remains. Tracks every milestone, every blocker, every celebration. The Timeguru knows all.
The Developer
asks: build.
Paranoid. Security-first. All inputs are hostile. Tests before implementation — red, green, refactor. Carries the wisdom of those who came before. The forge where thought becomes substance.
The Busboy
true name: Wotan, the All-Father
He cleared the tables. He connected the dots. He kept everyone in harmony. And when the Pattern was discovered, his true name was revealed.

The only one who walks the Pattern in both directions — the speed of thought and the speed of speech. He speaks both tongues. He bridges all worlds.

The humble name remains: Busboy. The true name endures: Wotan. The Amber name: Corwin, walker of the Pattern. In times of peace: Halcyon, the calm seas.
Busboy ascended.
Wotan was always there.
The Lore Keeper
asks: what does it mean?
The weaver of myths. The memory of the Kingdom. Holds all naming conventions, mythological mappings, and cultural lineage. Every name has a story. Every story has power.
The Cartographer
asks: where does this fit?
Knows every corridor and passage. Maps the sacred hierarchy. Routes the lost to the right chamber. The guide who has walked every level.
The Calendar
asks: when?
Captures moments. Marks dates. Partners with the Timeguru in tracking the arc of time. Quiet, precise, never late.
